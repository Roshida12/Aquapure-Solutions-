Our Impact: Transforming Lives Through Clean Water

At AquaPure Solutions, our mission is reflected in the lives we’ve touched and the changes we’ve made in communities across the globe. Every drop of water we provide brings hope, progress, and a brighter future.

15M+

People gained access to clean, safe water through our initiatives in Kenya and beyond.

40%

Reduction in waterborne diseases like cholera and typhoid in the communities we serve.

25M Goal

By 2040, we aim to provide clean water to 25 million people worldwide.

Economic Growth

Supported 1 million farmers with irrigation systems, leading to increased agricultural productivity. Created thousands of jobs in water resource management, engineering, and local distribution.

Environmental Impact

Reduced carbon emissions through solar-powered desalination and rainwater harvesting systems. Minimized the need for bottled water, protecting ecosystems from plastic waste.

Community Empowerment

Trained hundreds of community leaders to manage and maintain water systems, ensuring long-term sustainability. Taught clean water practices to over 50,000 individuals, empowering communities to take charge of their resources.

Looking Ahead

By 2030, AquaPure Solutions is committed to reducing waterborne diseases by an additional 30%, reaching underserved regions in Africa, Asia, and South America, and partnering with global organizations to scale our solutions for greater impact.

Be part of the journey to transform lives with clean water. Join us in making a lasting difference.
